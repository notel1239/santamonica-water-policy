# Santa Monica Sustainable Water Management Policy v1.0 - Officially Adopted

**Release Date:** December 28, 2025

## Summary

This release marks the official adoption of the Santa Monica Sustainable Water Management Policy. After a thorough drafting process and community feedback, the policy establishes comprehensive guidelines for residential, commercial, and municipal water use, aiming to ensure long-term water security and environmental sustainability.

## Key Goals

- Reduce per capita residential water consumption by 20% by 2030
- Promote rainwater harvesting and efficient fixtures
- Require water audits for large commercial properties
- Implement smart irrigation in public parks
- Lead by example through municipal water reduction targets
